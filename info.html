Map Information

A Champion World is a project built by HannoGeo, with the main goal being just a good distribution. All locations are generated with Vali.

The Distribution

As mentioned, the main reason that I even created this map is just because I hate the distributions of most other maps. For example, I think it's totally logical that the country with the most locs on a map is Russia. (people often say that's "ragebait" 🥀) Also, on most maps, countries with sparse coverage (Namibia, Kazakhstan, Bolivia, etc.) are massively underrepresented, which I am not a fan of. There are no badcam locs in countries where there's no need for them. (hopefully that could soon include India) I have worked on making the map as nice as possible, so enjoy :)

Updates

The map is updated regularly to include new areas as they are released in GeoGuessr. I also regularly review the map to fix any broken locations. If you do see any unofficial or broken coverage, feel free to let me know and I'll fix it.
